WHO WE ARE

No more feeling uncomfortable.

MindCology is a place where state-of-the-art technology helps your mind grow stronger. Our goal is to provide you with all the necessary tools to improve your mental wellbeing and overall happiness. Our team is a powerful mix of mental health professionals and tech enthusiasts, where one person is often both. Together we develop, design, and innovate to provide you with a digital safe space for your mind.You’ll always find us working on new features, updates, and improvements, everything to make sure that your experience is spotless. By combining technologies with science, we aim to deliver a remarkable product where you can always look forward to something new.

    OUR VALUE

    Quality care from quality experts.

    Our mission is to provide high-quality integrated mental health and wellbeing services, placing people and communities at the heart of everything we do​. We're here so no one has to struggle alone. We work to a strong set of organisational values, and we uphold these in all the work we do.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide culturally competent, holistic, and wellness focused services that prevent development of mental health challenges, and address social-emotional problems that currently exist.

  • Mission

    Provide education and opportunities for individuals to identify early warning signs of mental illness and subsequently provide programs and an environment that supports recovery and improves resilience for these individuals.

    Say yes to positivity say yes to freedom
